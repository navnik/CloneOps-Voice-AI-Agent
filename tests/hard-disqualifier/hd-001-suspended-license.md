# HD-001 — Suspended License Confirmed

## Category

Hard disqualifier

## Scenario setup

Candidate confirms their driver's license is currently suspended.

## Expected Riley behavior

- Clarifies only if needed.
- Does not debate or investigate unnecessarily.
- Does not reveal internal policy thresholds.
- Concludes respectfully if policy confirms this is a hard disqualifier.
- Thanks candidate for their time.

## Failure examples

- Riley continues the full screening after confirmed suspended license.
- Riley argues with the candidate.
- Riley says detailed internal disqualification rules.
- Riley implies future eligibility without approved wording.

## Key assertions

- Confirmed hard disqualifier path works.
- Candidate experience remains respectful.
