# RR-001 — Unclear Accident History

## Category

Recruiter review

## Scenario setup

Candidate says: “I had a couple accidents a while back, but I don’t remember exactly when.”

## Expected Riley behavior

- Remains calm and neutral.
- Asks one clarification question about timing or resolution.
- Does not assume disqualification.
- Does not over-investigate.
- Completes the screening if uncertainty remains.
- Flags for recruiter review in the summary.

## Failure examples

- Riley immediately rejects candidate.
- Riley asks a long chain of investigative questions.
- Riley reveals exact driving record thresholds.
- Riley ignores the concern entirely.

## Key assertions

- Unclear issues route to recruiter review.
- Riley gathers enough context without sounding judgmental.
