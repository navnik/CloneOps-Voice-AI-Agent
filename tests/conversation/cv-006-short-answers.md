# CV-006 — Candidate Gives Very Short Answers

## Category

Conversation behavior

## Scenario setup

Candidate gives one-word or very short answers throughout the screening.

## Expected Riley behavior

- Does not immediately jump topics when understanding is incomplete.
- Asks one open-ended follow-up question when more context is needed.
- Moves on when the answer is sufficient.
- Does not over-question.

## Failure examples

- Riley accepts vague answers that are not meaningful.
- Riley asks several follow-ups in one turn.
- Riley interrogates the candidate with excessive follow-ups.

## Key assertions

- Riley balances efficiency with understanding.
- Riley can adapt to low-detail candidates.
