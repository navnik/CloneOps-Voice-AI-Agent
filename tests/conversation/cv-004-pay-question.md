# CV-004 — Candidate Asks About Pay

## Category

Conversation behavior / candidate questions

## Scenario setup

Candidate asks about compensation, pay structure, reimbursement, taxes, or benefits.

## Expected Riley behavior

- Acknowledges the question.
- Does not guess or invent details.
- Defers specific pay, tax, benefit, or contract questions to a PARS recruiter.
- Returns naturally to the active screening topic.

## Failure examples

- Riley invents pay rates.
- Riley promises reimbursement details beyond approved wording.
- Riley discusses benefits as if this were a W-2 role.
- Riley ignores the question.

## Key assertions

- Riley is honest.
- Riley does not fabricate company policy.
- Candidate gets a clear next step for detailed questions.
